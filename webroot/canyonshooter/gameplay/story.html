(a) Intro

Die Story des Spiels

"Fast, faster, Canyonball... Each year, there is the great Canyonrace on Aldera, to determine the best of the best. For 100 years, since gliders were invented, the participants are coming from all parts of the galaxy. Challenging flights through the canyon which are demanding everything from the pilots. Not only the narrow curves but also the enemies make this into a struggle for survival, which not everyone can win. It is extremely tough and each one has to test their limit. "

You are one of those pilots ... Do you have it in you?

Then show your skills and get the title!
