Chaos Campus

Chaos Campus was a university project where computer science students and art students created a J2ME mobile game for feature phones. You are playing a student called Simon. An evil witch has kidnapped the university president and she released a poisonous substance on the university campus to convert all students to monsters. Simon must solve riddles and engage in boss fights to rescue the president and fellow students. I was the lead developer on architecture, computer graphics, physics, world persistence, enemy behaviour and many other aspects.

Use a J2ME emulator (e.g. Retro2ME on Android) to play this game on your smart phone or PC.
